The Four Digital Horsemen

Disinformation poses a real threat to the truth because it is spread quickly and widely, especially on social media platforms. Every individual can now become an information producer, creating a huge volume of information that is difficult for ordinary users to filter.

Disinformation

Intentionally false information created to cause harm.

Misinformation

False information spread, regardless of intent to mislead.

Fake News

False stories that appear to be news, spread on the internet or using other media.

Pseudoscience

Beliefs or practices mistakenly regarded as being based on scientific method.
